Stella: Real or Not?

November 15, 2020

Phil, the Park Ranger, told me that there’s no one working in his department named Stella. He also told me that no one would be crazy enough to be around Cerberus on the night of the full moon, and not to try it again.

That's not the point here, though.

The point is that I met a woman who the entire town she supposedly lives in and her supposed employer says does not exist. I didn’t meet her in any malicious context, no, in fact she saved me.

It’s just that she lied to me about her status as an official and her name and everything about herself in a moment of vulnerability.

I didn’t see Stella again last night, but as I was leaving this morning I heard one of the rangers mumbling about missing a uniform from inventory. So...
